HOW TO COOK MY FAVOURITE MEAL


EGUSI SOUP


INGREDIENTS:

  • 2 cups of ground Egusi (melon seeds).
  • 1kg of assorted meat (beef, goat meat, shaki, kpomo).
  • 1-2 big dried fish and stock fish(head and Flakes).
  • 1 cup of palm oil.
  • 3 table spoons of ground crayfish.
  • 2 seasoning cubes (knor).
  • Salt to taste.
  • 2-3 cups of water.
  • 1 tea spoon of pepper(dry or fresh to taste).
  • 1 small onion.
  • Uziza leaf(washed and shredded).

STEPS

  1. Wash and season the (assorted meats, stock fish, Dry Fish, Kpomo) with salt, seasoning cubes, onions and water.
  2. Allow it to boil like 40minutes.
  3. Use another pot pour palm oil and heat with medium heat.
  4. Pour your sliced onoins and stir.
  5. Pour your ground Egusi and stir
  6. Mix the fried Egusi with your seasoned meat,dry fish,stock fish,kpomo.
  7. Add your pepper and salt to taste.
  8. Allow it to boil for 5-10 minutes.
  9. Add little water if is too thick.
  10. Add your washed shredded Uziza leaf and allow it to boil for 2 minuts.
  11. Food is Ready

SUMMARY

Egusi soup is a Nigerian soup made with ground melon seeds,palm oil, assorted meats, and vegetables.

Egusi soup is rich and delicious.

It is commonly eaten with pounded yam, eba, fufu or semovita.
